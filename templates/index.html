About Shankh

Shankh is an integrated platform designed to strengthen ocean hazard reporting and response. We combine community participation, real-time data, and social media analytics to build a faster, more reliable coastal safety system. By empowering citizens and enabling collaboration with authorities, Shankh ensures timely action, better preparedness, and improved protection for India’s coastal communities.

Instant Hazard Reporting

Citizens can quickly report incidents such as cyclones, flooding, unusual tides, and accidents with geo-tagged details and media uploads.

Real-Time Analytics

Reports are processed and analyzed alongside social media signals to identify credible threats and emerging patterns.

Accessible Communication

Multilingual support and an easy-to-use interface ensure inclusivity for coastal communities across India.

Decision Support

Verified hazard data helps officials and disaster management teams respond faster and make informed decisions to protect lives and livelihoods.

Our Mission

At Shankh, our mission is to create a community-powered coastal safety network where every observation matters. We aim to turn citizen reports into actionable insights, helping disaster management teams respond quickly and effectively. By harnessing the power of collective intelligence and technology, we work to safeguard lives, livelihoods, and ecosystems along India’s vast coastline.
